7 Proven Methods to Reduce No-Shows

Stop losing money to empty chairs. Implement these simple strategies and reduce no-shows by up to 70%.

1. Automated Reminders

Send automatic SMS & email reminders 24 hours before the appointment. This is the single most effective way to prevent no-shows.

Impact: 38-50% Reduction

2. Require Confirmation

Ask clients to click a button to confirm they're coming. It creates a small but powerful psychological commitment.

Impact: 25-35% Reduction

3. Cancellation Policy

Clearly state your cancellation policy (e.g., 24-hour notice). Even without fees, it sets a professional tone and expectation.

Impact: 20-30% Reduction

4. Collect Deposits

Requiring a small, refundable deposit at the time of booking dramatically increases the show-up rate.

Impact: 30-50% Reduction

5. Easy Rescheduling

Provide a one-click link in reminders to reschedule. People are less likely to no-show if they can easily move the appointment.

Impact: 15-25% Reduction

6. Follow Up on No-Shows

Send a friendly, automated "We missed you!" message. It helps re-engage clients and discourages repeat offenses.

Impact: Improves Future Attendance

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I already have a website?+

That's perfect! You can easily add your ProBookingQR link or QR code to your existing site with a "Book Now" button. This system is designed to complement what you already have, not replace it. It's a lightweight, dedicated booking engine.

Do I need a website to offer online booking?+

No, you don't need a website for QR code booking. The system creates a standalone booking page that customers access by scanning your QR code. You can display the QR code anywhere - business cards, tables, social media, or print materials.

When should I send appointment reminders?+

We recommend sending the first reminder 24 hours before the appointment and a second, final reminder 2 hours before. Our system automates this for you, and we've found this two-reminder system is the most effective at reducing no-shows without annoying clients.

Can I block off personal time?+

Yes. Since ProBookingQR syncs directly with your Google Calendar, all you have to do is create an event in your calendar for your personal time (like "Doctor's Appointment" or "Lunch"). Our system will see that you're busy and won't show that time as available for booking.

Should I charge no-show fees?+

It's up to you, but we recommend starting without them. Our automated reminders and confirmation requests reduce no-shows so effectively that fees often aren't necessary. If you do want to charge fees, you can state that in your booking policies.

What's the cheapest way to start taking online bookings?+

QR code booking systems like ours are typically the most affordable option. They require no large setup costs, no developer fees, and no expensive website modifications. Our flat monthly fee covers everything.
